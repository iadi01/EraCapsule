Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. At EraCapsule, we collect only the information necessary to deliver your letters to your future self. This includes your email address, message content, and the date you select for delivery.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your letters remain private and are only accessible to you. Messages are stored securely on our servers until they are sent on your chosen date.

If you choose to make your letter public, it may appear on our website anonymously, but no personal data (like your name or email) will ever be shown.

You may request deletion of your stored letters or account data at any time by contacting us at support@gmail.com. Once deleted, your information cannot be recovered.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but no online service is completely risk-free. By using EraCapsule, you acknowledge that you understand and accept this risk.

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our service means you accept those updates.
