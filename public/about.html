About EraCapsule

EraCapsule is a heartfelt web app designed to help you connect with your future self. You can write a message, letter, or reminder — and we’ll deliver it to your inbox on the date you choose.

Whether it’s a reflection, a goal, or a note of hope, this platform is made to inspire you to look back, reflect on your journey, and see how far you’ve come.

Our mission is simple — to help people grow emotionally, remember their dreams, and appreciate their progress through time.
